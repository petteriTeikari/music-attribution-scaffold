## Narrative Position

**How the Chat Interface Works: Sound Becomes Understanding**

This figure replaces the clichéd "vinyl groove spiral" with an anatomically accurate and musically relevant metaphor: the cochlea in the inner ear. Just as the cochlea's spiral processes sound waves into neural signals the brain can understand, the chat interface processes artist input into structured attribution data.

---

## Why Should I Care?

> "This is how your words become verified credits."

## Explain It to a Label Exec

> "The chat is like your inner ear—it takes the 'sound' of your input and transforms it into structured data the system can understand and verify."
